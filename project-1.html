Project Overview

The 3D Model Customizer is an open-source tool for real-time customization of 3D models in the browser. It offers an intuitive interface, responsive design, and optimized performance. Users can easily modify colors, textures, shapes, and sizes.

Modify various aspects of 3D models in real-time, including colors, textures, shapes, and sizes. A clean and intuitive UI ensures that even users with minimal experience in 3D modeling can navigate and use the tool effectively. Efficiently handles complex 3D models without compromising on performance, thanks to optimized rendering techniques. Being open-source, the project encourages collaboration and contributions from the community to enhance its functionality and features.
